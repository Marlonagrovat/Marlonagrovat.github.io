## Overview

A personal portfolio site for **Nkemjika "Jude" Okeke**, Data & Business Analyst — MBA holder with a decade of cross-sector experience spanning banking, manufacturing, logistics, agriculture, healthcare, and SME consulting.

The site frames every project around three questions: *What is the problem? What does the data reveal? What should the business do next?* — positioning the work as decision-ready analysis rather than passive reporting.
